## Inverzne funkcije kotnih funkcij

$$\sin^{-1}(\alpha) = \arcsin(\alpha)$$
$$\cos^{-1}(\alpha) = \arccos(\alpha)$$
$$\tan^{-1}(\alpha) = \arctan(\alpha)$$

$$\tan = \frac{\sin}{\cos}$$
$$\cot = \frac{\cos}{\sin}$$
